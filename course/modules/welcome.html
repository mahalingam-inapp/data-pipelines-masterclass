Data Pipelines Masterclass

From ingestion and ETL to storage and analytics — a progressive, practice-oriented curriculum.

22 Modules
7 Sections

What you'll learn

This course walks you through the full data pipeline lifecycle: why pipelines matter, how to ingest and transform data (including the medallion architecture), how to manage quality and staging, where to store data (warehouses, lakes, marts, cubes), and how to connect everything to analytics with Power BI and Tableau. Each section builds from basics and ends with a practical use case. The final section is a capstone plus failure simulation and roadmap.

Course structure

Section 1: Introduction & Purpose (Modules 1–3)
What is a data pipeline; why they matter; roles; use case: From Spreadsheets to Pipeline.
Section 2: Data Ingestion (Modules 4–6)
Ingestion fundamentals; connectors, APIs, reliability; use case: Ingest and Land.
Section 3: ETL & Medallion (Modules 7–9)
ETL fundamentals; medallion architecture & incremental; use case: Medallion in Practice.
Section 4: Data Management (Modules 10–12)
Engineering practices; cleaning, validation & quality; use case: From Raw to Curated.
Section 5: Storage (Modules 13–15)
Storage types; warehouses, lakes, marts & cubes; use case: Storage Strategy.
Section 6: Analytics (Modules 16–19)
Pipeline to analytics; Power BI; Tableau; use case: End-to-End Report.
Section 7: Capstone & Simulation (Modules 20–22)
Full pipeline capstone; simulation & holistic view; roadmap & next steps.
For technical architects, data engineers, and analysts. Role-specific callouts and activities are included so you can tailor depth to your role (design and standards vs. implementation vs. consumption and reporting).
