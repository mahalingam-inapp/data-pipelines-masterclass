In plain terms: You’ve covered the full pipeline lifecycle. Next steps depend on your role and goals: go deeper on streaming, ML pipelines, data mesh, or tool-specific certifications. Where to go next

  • Streaming — Real-time ingestion and processing (event streams, windowing, exactly-once).

  • Data mesh — Domain-oriented ownership, product thinking, federated governance.
  • Tooling — Deeper dbt, orchestration (e.g. Airflow), cloud data services, or BI certifications.
